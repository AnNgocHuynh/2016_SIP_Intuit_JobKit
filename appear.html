Looking Your Best...

...is important. Your appearance at an interview is your potential employer's first impression of you. By presenting a profession and clean appearance, you can ingrain a sense of confidence in your ability to complete the the tasks that your employer is looking for. The following suggestions of attire can serve you as potential guidelines:

  • Clean, polished, and conservative dress shoes
  • Well-groomed hairstyle
  • Cleaned and trimmed fingernails
  • Minimal cologne or perfume
  • Minimal jewelry
